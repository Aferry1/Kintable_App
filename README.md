# Kintable_App
Group project for the Project Management course at Maryville University

Group members: Adam, Jack, Rida, Madyssen

## Product Vision
For military families and busy parents who struggle with decision fatigue, social isolation, and food waste caused by frequent relocation and time constraints, KinTable is a hyper-local meal-sharing platform that enables households to share or sell extra portions of home-cooked meals. The platform addresses the everyday problem of “leftover inefficiency,” where cooking for small families often produces unused food and wasted ingredients. Unlike traditional food delivery services such as DoorDash, Uber Eats, and Grubhub, KinTable introduces a “village-as-a-service” model that transforms extra servings into opportunities for neighborhood connection, mutual support, and reduced household waste.
